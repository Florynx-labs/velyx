# VELYX Visual Identity & Brand Guidelines

Developed by **Florynx Labs**, VELYX embodies precision engineering, high-performance compilation, and aesthetic elegance.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette

| Token Name | Hex Value | RGB / Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deep Space (Background)** | `#07090E` | Main dark background |
| **Velyx Indigo (Accent Primary)** | `#6366F1` | Primary CTA buttons & active states |
| **Signal Cyan (Accent Light)** | `#38BDF8` | Reactive signals & metrics |
| **Emerald Flash (Success)** | `#34D399` | Benchmark highlights & status |
| **Card Surface** | `#12161F` | Elevated containers & playground panels |

---

## ✒️ Typography
* **Primary Sans-Serif:** Inter (`font-family: 'Inter', system-ui, sans-serif;`)
* **Monospace Code:** JetBrains Mono (`font-family: 'JetBrains Mono', monospace;`)

---

## 📐 Logo & Badge Usage
* Always maintain clear padding equal to 50% of logo height around brand marks.
* Prefer dark mode placement on `#07090E` background with subtle indigo glow.
